Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM AZAD

The Misile Man Of India

Here is a time line of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam life

  • 15 oct 1931 - Born in Rameshwaram, India
  • 1958 - Joined Defence Research and Devlopment Organization
  • 1982 - India’s first indigenously designed and produced satellite launch vehicle. Rejoining DRDO
  • 1992-1997 - Kalam was scientific adviser to the defense minister
  • 1999-2001 - He later served as principal scientific adviser to the government with the rank of cabinet minister
  • 1998 - Kalam put forward a countrywide plan called Technology Vision 2020
  • 2002 - India’s ruling National Democratic Alliance (NDA) put forward Kalam to succeed outgoing President Kocheril Raman Narayanan.
  • 2002 to 2007 - President Of India
  • WON Bharat Ratna for his great work
  • 2015 Died in Shillong
